Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add players to the game?

On the "Enter Player Names" screen, type each player’s name into the text fields. You need at least two players to start. Tap "Start Game" when ready.

Can I switch decks mid-game?

No, but you can restart the game from the scoreboard by selecting "New Game (Choose Deck)" and picking a different deck, including the family-friendly option.

What happens if I skip a challenge?

Skipping adds strikes (1-3 based on difficulty). The first player to 30 strikes loses the game.

Why isn’t my app loading?

Ensure you’re on iOS 17.2 or later, check your internet connection (for initial setup), and restart the app. If issues persist, contact us.

Troubleshooting

  • App Crashes: Update to the latest version from the App Store and restart your device.
  • Text Fields Hard to Read: This was fixed in v1.0.1—update if you’re on an older version.
  • Game Won’t Start: Ensure at least two player names are entered without duplicates.
