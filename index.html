Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add players to the game?

On the "Enter Player Names" screen, type each player’s name into the text fields. You need at least two players to start. Tap "Start Game" when ready.

Can I switch decks mid-game?

No, but you can restart the game from the scoreboard by selecting "New Game (Choose Deck)" and picking a different deck.

What happens if I skip a challenge?

Skipping adds strikes (1-3 based on difficulty). The first player to 30 strikes loses the game.

Why isn’t my app loading?

Ensure you’re on iOS 17.2 or later, check your internet connection (for initial setup), and restart the app. If issues persist, contact us.

Troubleshooting

  • App Crashes: Update to the latest version from the App Store and restart your device.
  • Text Fields Hard to Read: This was fixed in v1.0.1—update if you’re on an older version.
  • Game Won’t Start: Ensure at least two player names are entered without duplicates.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 26, 2025

1. Introduction

DareDeck ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This section explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS app, DareDeck.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to ensure a smooth gaming experience:

  • Player Names: Temporarily stored locally on your device when you enter names for gameplay. We don’t upload or store this data on our servers.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics (e.g., app opens, crashes) via Apple’s standard tools to improve performance. No personally identifiable information is collected.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use data solely to:

  • Enable gameplay features (e.g., tracking player strikes).
  • Analyze app performance and fix bugs.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties, except:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data with Apple for app insights.

5. Data Security

Your player names stay on your device and are deleted when you uninstall the app. We use industry-standard practices to secure analytics data.

6. Children’s Privacy

DareDeck offers decks for all ages but includes adult content (e.g., NSFW decks). It’s not intended for children under 13 without parental consent. We don’t knowingly collect data from minors under 13.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete player names by restarting the game.
  • Contact us at support@daredeck.com with privacy questions.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Check here for the latest version. Significant changes will be noted in the app.

9. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, email support@daredeck.com.
